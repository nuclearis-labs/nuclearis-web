The first ASME in Latin America

Certified to manufacture mechanical components class 1

NPT is the type of certification that guarantees Nuclearis adequacy to manufacture Class 1 mechanical components, which are installed in the core of nuclear power plants and which, due to their critical importance, require the highest quality, traceability and control requirements (these quality standards are even more rigorous than the standards that apply to the aerospace industry). This prestigious"stamp," NPT, not only enables us to manufacture Class 1 mechanical parts but also export them with extensive added value to any country in the world. Moreover, it enables us to start manufacturing mechanical components for Carem-25, a Small Modular reactor that is currently under construction in our country, Argentina.
