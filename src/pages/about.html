- Our Vision

To be a leading company in the Argentine nuclear industry with a strong projection to international atomic energy markets.

- Our Mission

To provide state-of-the-art technological solutions for the global nuclear industry based on continuous innovation and commitment to quality.

Nuclear Inspiration

At Nuclearis, we are committed not only to developing engineering solutions, we also specialize in manufacturing complex mechanical equipment for the nuclear industry.

Our methodology consists of the integration of various projects with separate suppliers. We then ship the different components to our production plant where these are assembled. All our operations are conducted under a robust quality assurance program meeting the requirements of ASME III certification and ISO9001.

History Line

History Line

2008

Nuclearis detects an opportunity within the nuclear industry.

2009

Nuclearis develops the necessary technology to manufacture nuclear seal rings.

2010

Nuclearis obtains ISO 9001 Certification.

2011

Nuclearis provides the nuclear power plant Atucha I with nuclear seal rings for the first time.

2012

Nuclearis produces other mechanical components for Atucha I.

2013

Nuclearis opens its new offices in San Carlos de Bariloche, Neuquen, Argentina. Nuclearis develops the basic necessary technology for the nuclear reactor CAREM 2.

2014

Nuclearis supplies the nuclear seal rings necessary to set up the nuclear power plant Atucha II.

2015

Nuclearis opens its engineering development offices in Nodus Business District. Nuclearis develops the detailed engineering for the reactor CAREM II.

2016

Nuclearis develops and manufactures mechanical parts for various projects.

2017

Nuclearis assembles a team of experts to obtain ASME III Nuclear Certification. Nuclearis created a team to develop a manufacturing process tracking software based on Blockchain technology.

2018

Nuclearis sets up NUCLEARIS CORPORATION in New Mexico, USA.

2019

Nuclearis sets up a new manufacturing plant in Vicente López, Buenos Aires province, Argentina.

2020

Nuclearis installs different production lines to manufacture various new nuclear mechanical components. Nuclearis launches NUCLEAR STRACK, a Blockchain software platform to track the manufacturing of nuclear mechanical components.

2021

Nuclearis obtains ASME III Nuclear Certification types NPT and MO. Nuclearis develops and launches MATTERIA, an ASME-compliant documentation management platform based on Blockchain technology.

2022

Nuclearis sets up the first manufacturing plant in Latin America to produce Cobalt 59 Gamma source radiation disks. Nuclearis launches DOSETRACK, a Blockchain software platform for monitoring absorbed doses from radioactive sources in the industrial gammagraphy area.
