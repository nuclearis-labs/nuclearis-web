Quality Assurance

Since its origins in 2009, NUCLEARIS has been ISO 9001 certified for "Manufacturing of mechanical components for the nuclear industry and engineering project management". Furthermore, we have also obtained the ASME Nuclear- BPVC SECTION III Certification type NPT Certificate for class 1,2 &3 fabrication without design responsibility ans as a Material Organization for ferrous and non-ferrous material" from the American Society of Mechanical Engineers. The implementation of these quality system programs not only guarantees the safety of our products but also provides assurance that NUCLEARIS’ manufacturing processes are performed in accordance with the nuclear industry requirements, the international legal ones and our customers.’ Due to the successful application of such quality systems, NUCLEARIS is able to deal with a large amount of documentation on a daily basis since it requires exhaustive planning, definition of tasks and responsibilities, recording of obtained results and guideline development for continuous internal inspections. In addition, by selecting suppliers who meet the specific criteria of such quality standards, NUCLEARIS has managed to build a strong and reliable supply chain network.
