Manufacturing

Our highly skilled team is composed of a remarkable group of engineers from different disciplines with extensive experience and training in automatic machinery design, high-precision mechanical components, complex robotized mechanisms, metal 3D printing and electrolytic coatings of special alloys.
Our engineers manage the projects following the procedures of our quality assurance management system meticulously and provide appropriate solutions in each case:
· Identification of needs and development of the conceptual design of the machinery to be created.
· 3D modeling, calculation, simulation and detail engineering.
· Mechanical parts production outsourcing to qualified suppliers and manufacturing process tracking.
· Mechanical assembly and set-up as well as automation execution.
· Technical support and warranty service
